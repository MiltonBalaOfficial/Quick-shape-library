Quick Shape Library along with Extract Stroke Info:

1. Place the folder (Quick Shape Library) in C:\Users\your user name\AppData\Local\xournalpp\plugins
	AppData folder may be hidden.
2. Place the icons ("shapes_symbolic" and "extract-info-symbolic")  in C:\Program Files\Xournal++\share\icons\Adwaita\symbolic\actions
3. In Xournal++ app, go to customize toolbars>Plugins, and place the icons at a suitable place
4. use it.

Add your own shape with Extract Stroke Info:

1. Draw your own Shapes. Place it on the left corner (because the shape will be inserted at the same point and if the page be smaller than
 the current you may not be able to see the inserted shapes)
2. Select your shape and just hit the "ESI" icon or select the plugin from plugin menu.
3. Your shape is saved at the plugin folder, name: giveMeName_and_placeMe_in_shapesFolder.lua
4. Just rename it and place it in the "Shapes" subfolder 
5. Now little bit coding: open "quick_shape_library.lua" in code editor, focus on "The dictionary for shapes ", you will understand everything

Dont forget to share your unique shapes with us!
