1. Paste the folder (Quick Shape Library) in C:\Users\<user>\AppData\Local\xournalpp\plugins\
    <user> is your user name, AppData may be hidden (just unhide)
2. paste the icon (shapes_symbolic)  in C:\Program Files\Xournal++\share\icons\Adwaita\symbolic\actions
3. In Xournal++ app, go to customize toolbars>Plugins, and place the icon at a suitable place
5. use
